<h3><i class="fas fa-user-graduate"></i> Research Profile (English)</h3>
<div class="about-block about-block-en">
I currently work at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing University of Technology, where I teach and conduct research in computer systems. My research interests encompass <span style="color:#1f6feb;"><strong>computer architecture, real-time systems, real-time distributed services (RT-DDS), autonomous driving systems, and embodied intelligent systems</strong></span>. Focusing on <span style="color:#1f6feb;"><strong>formal modeling, timing analysis, and system-level optimization of cyber-physical systems (CPS) and real-time systems</strong></span>, I systematically study the deterministic execution and predictable performance of complex computing systems in heterogeneous resource environments. My research emphasizes key technologies such as resource scheduling and allocation mechanisms, real-time communication and data consistency guarantees, system schedulability analysis, and their engineering implementation. My research addresses <span style="color:#1f6feb;"><strong>safety-critical and high-real-time application scenarios such as autonomous driving, robotics systems, and satellite systems, aiming to provide theoretically sound and engineeringly feasible real-time assurance methods and system optimization schemes for complex autonomous systems</strong></span>.
</div>

<h3><i class="fas fa-user"></i> 研究简介（中文）</h3>
<div class="about-block about-block-zh">
我目前任职于北京工业大学计算机学院，从事计算机系统方向的教学与科研工作。我的研究方向涵盖<span style="color:#1f6feb;"><strong>计算机体系结构、实时系统、实时分布式服务（RT-DDS）、自动驾驶系统与具身智能系统</strong></span>等领域。
围绕信息物理系统（Cyber-Physical Systems, CPS）与实时系统的<span style="color:#1f6feb;"><strong>形式化建模、时序分析与系统级优化</strong></span>这一核心主线，我系统研究复杂计算系统在异构资源环境下的执行确定性与性能可预测性问题。
研究重点包括资源调度与分配机制、实时通信与数据一致性保障、系统可调度性分析及其工程化实现等关键技术。
相关研究面向<span style="color:#1f6feb;"><strong>自动驾驶、机器人系统与卫星系统等安全关键与强实时应用场景</strong></span>，致力于为复杂自主系统提供具有理论支撑和工程可落地性的实时性保障方法与系统优化方案。
</div>
